The plot of the game

The events in the game take place in the Magic Forest of Soultern.The mission of our main character Ganyo is to beat the big boss at the end of the game and to return to the real world.The game has four levels with different specifics and on each level there is one boss, which the player must kill so that he/she can continue playing.

Explore some of the Soultern's levels

Summer:

You continue your journey and after hearing strange noises you make your first friend in the game.

Fall:

While travelling on a steamship, the Beast takes over the level's boss and kidnaps your friend.

Winter:

As the winter is the last season of the year this may very well be the end of your adventure.
